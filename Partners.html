Our Partners

The organizers are putting together TEDxIUT thanks to the generous help of a select group of enterprises and organizations committed to sustainability and the power of ideas. A warm token of gratitude for their support, despite the inability of sponsors’ presence on the stage (no logo / name or appearance) during the event, catering to the sponsorship guidelines set by TED. The uniqueness of sponsoring TEDxIUT is in finding some of the best hand-picked diverse attendees, movers & shakers under one roof. Most partners help us with in-kind product or service supplies with partial or full waivers. All funds of TEDxIUT goes back to developing the event itself, the organizers don’t take a salary or make a profit from TEDxIUT.
